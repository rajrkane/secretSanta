Secret Santa Generator

Directions

  1. Enter the name of each group member separated by commas (e.g. "Alice, Bob, Carol, Dave, Eve"), ensuring that no names are repeated and that there are more than 2 participants.
  2. Click the button to generate a text file for each person containg their assignment.
  3. Download the text files and send each one to the corresponding group member.
